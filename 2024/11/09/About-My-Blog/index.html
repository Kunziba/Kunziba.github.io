A general introduction to my blog and something to say.

Content

My blog mainly consists of my notes and my lecture-notes (just for studying and sharing), including subjects such as maths (for physics), physics, philosophy, etc. You can get a panorama to them by choosing categories you like ( Note& Lecture Note) or simply browse them on the homepage. If you want to have a view of a specific subject, you can select them by choosing a tag interested. In addition to notes and lecture-notes related to my course and major, you can also find my basic information and recent activities and some essays in my blog, and if you want to discuss some topics mentioned with me, contact with me by the social media account provided on the homepage. At the same time, if you think my notes or something else are of help to you, tipping is welcomed.
